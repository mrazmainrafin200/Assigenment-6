About the Campaign

Green Earth is a global tree plantation initiative dedicated to fighting climate change. Since our start, we’ve planted over 500,000 trees worldwide. By joining our campaign, you help restore forests, create habitats for wildlife, and combat global warming. Together, we can make a lasting impact on our planet. Join us in planting a tree today for a greener tomorrow.

  • Restoration of natural habitats
  • Improvement of air quality
  • Support for local communities
